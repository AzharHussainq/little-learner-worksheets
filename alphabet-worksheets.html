Teaching Alphabet to Preschoolers

Start with Letter Recognition

Begin by teaching children to recognize letters before focusing on writing them. Use fun games and matching activities.

Teach Letter Sounds

Introduce letter sounds alongside letter names. This helps with early reading skills and phonics development.

Multi-Sensory Approach

Combine tracing, coloring, and tactile activities like sandpaper letters or playdough to reinforce learning.
